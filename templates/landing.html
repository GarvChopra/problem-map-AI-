Features

Live Heatmap

Real-time map of every civic issue across Delhi, color-coded by severity and updated as reports stream in.

How it works

From a photo to a fix in 4 steps.

Total effort for a citizen: 1 photo, 1 tap. AreaPulse handles the rest.

01

Snap a photo

Citizen takes a photo of the civic issue with their phone — pothole, garbage, broken streetlight, anything.

02

AI classifies

Vision AI reads the photo, identifies the category, scores severity, and writes a description automatically.

03

Auto-routed

Spam check passes. Report saves to Firestore. AI drafts a formal email to the right authority for the area.

04

Track resolution

Citizens watch their report move through verification, escalation, and resolution — earning points along the way.

About

Civic complaints aren't broken. The system around them is.

Delhi citizens file thousands of complaints every year — potholes, garbage, broken streetlights, water leaks. They're scattered across disconnected portals (MCD, DJB, BSES, e-District) with no follow-up, no spam filtering, and no accountability.

AreaPulse fixes the layer between citizens and authority. AI does the heavy lifting at every stage: classification, spam detection, severity scoring, dispatch drafting, and citywide pattern detection. Citizens stop feeling unheard. Authorities get clean, structured signals. NGOs find issues they can act on.
